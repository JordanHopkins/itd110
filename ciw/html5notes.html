HTML5 Body Parts

Header: Defines the top of the Web page; typically includes the company logo. Main: Defines content that is directly related to the subject of the document Nav: Defines an area for navigation links; typically includes links to other resources on the site. Section: Defines portions or areas of a document; typically consist of a group of articles or images. Article: Defines site content accompanying the main content; typically includes company services, blogs, imgaes tweets, ans so on. Aside: Defines content that is supplementary to the main content; typically includes advertisements or news feeds. Footer: Defines the bottom of the Web page; typically includes copyright and cotact information.
