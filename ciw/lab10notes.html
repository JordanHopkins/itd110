Designers must also consider that the current and future generations of mobile devices are equipped with high-definition image capabilities. However, there are guidelines that you can follow to make your images as responsive as possible. In general, images (including icons) should be as flexible as possible so that graphics do not appear blurry on devices with high-pixel density due to poor scaling. To this end, you can use the mobile design technique of proportional image resizing, which was discussed earlier in this lesson. You can also use the responsive design technique of fluid images.
